TEAHOUSE WEBSITE REBRANDING

Rebranding of the TeaHouse site. Gained insight into designing for a service.

Client: TeaHouse
Team: 3 Product Designers
Time Constraint: 1 Month
Tags: Service Design, Cuisine, Art

Our team decided to capture the spirit of Tea House by creating a modern and user-friendly website. This platform enables both new and returning customers to delve into the authenticity and rich history of this cherished Chinese restaurant.

BRAINSTORMING STAGE

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

Sidewalk Kitchen

Pros: Large hero (but too big), has good images, scrollable

Cons: No real header, unorganized, outdated, looks "cheap"

Lepot Chinese Hotspot

Pros: Has images, lots of information

Cons: Huge ad on top, unorganized, "unofficial website", outdated, hard to navigate

Shuang Cheng

Pros: Effective color scheme, shows off ambiance

Cons: Not enough emphasis on food, awkward welcome section, header is cramped, lower images serve no purpose
